Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift is an American singer and composer. Swift's music spans across multiple genres, including pop, country, rock, and electronic. She began professional songwriting around 2005 at the age of 14, with her debut album Taylor Swift releasing in 2006. Swift is currently touring globally, with major economic impact. It's one of the highest grossing music events that have ever existed, with estimated projection of $2.17 billion by the end of the tour in 2024.
